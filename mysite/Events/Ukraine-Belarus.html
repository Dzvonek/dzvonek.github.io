    Activation of Small and Medium Business in the Border Regions of Ukraine-Belarus

    In the framework of the implementation of the project “Activation of Small and Medium Business in the Border Regions of Ukraine-Belarus” on the EaPTC Program financed by the European Union, the Slavutych Municipal Enterprise Support Fund and the Slavutych Executive Committee, on December 6, 2018, in the premises of the Belarusian Trade and Economic University of Consumer Cooperatives were organized and held the III Inter-sectoral meeting: housing and communal services and information and technology.

    The purpose of the event was to establish a constructive and transparent dialogue between the business of Ukraine and Belarus.

    our members of our company were invited to the Gomel. They visited the Gomel Technopark, where they were met with the director, Morozov Dmitry, who gave a presentation about the Gomel Technopark and the activities of residents. During the presentation, a lively dialogue arose between the participants of the meeting and the administration of Technopark. Representatives of state bodies and private enterprises of Ukraine were able to discuss the prospects for further cooperation with residents, as well as exchange professional experience and ideas.

    Our members performed very well at the seminar, after excursion, where they clarified the issue of the development of IT companies in a small town.
